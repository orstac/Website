About Us

Welcome to OrsTac, your number one source for websites, mc servers, and other development things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best websites, mc servers, and other things, with a focus on dedication, quality, and coffee.

Founded in 2019 by Xestra, OrsTac has come a long way from its beginnings in Pittsburgh. When Xestra first started out, his passion for development drove them to make OrsTac so that OrsTac can offer you some of the best development products. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
